The author.

Thank you for visiting Fly-Fiction. I started this project in the spring of 2020 when the Covid-19 pandemic began. Learning to build a website, produce audio, and continuing to write has been a lasting and rewarding experience. Thank you to everyone who has supported me along the way.

This project draws inspiration from fly fishing on rivers in Oregon and Washington. There will be more stories and poems added soon along with other web features that I am working on. Eventually I hope to explore more of the West beyond my corner in the PNW.

Everything here-the words, photographs, audio-are original work and I ask that you do not reproduce any of it without my written permission. Please send me any comments, feedback, and questions you have. I would love to hear from you. You can reach me at zach (at) flyfiction.com

If you would like to go fishing sometime, send a note.

More to come soon.

Zach
