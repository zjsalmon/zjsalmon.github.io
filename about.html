The author.

Thank you for visiting Fly-Fiction. I started this project in the spring of 2020 when the Covid-19 pandemic began. Learning to build a website, produce audio, and begin new writing projects has been meaningful during a time in life filled with uncertainty.

Fly-Fiction will continue to expand and explore rivers throughout the West. Everything here-the words, photographs, audio-are original work and I ask that you do not reproduce them without my permission. Please send me a note with any comments, feedback, and questions you have. I would love to hear from you. You can reach me at zach (at) flyfiction.com

More to come soon.

Zach
